# ‘Abdu’l-Bahá Abbas Arrived on a Mission

Putnam Connecticut Patriot  
April 19, 1912  
Putnam, CT  



‘Abdu’l-Bahá Abbas, a noted Persian prophet, arrived on a mission to bring about world peace and a unification of all religions.
