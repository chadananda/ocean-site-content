# A Barbecue for ‘Abdu’l-Bahá {.title}

The Sun  
June 28, 1912  
Morris Plains, NJ
{.noid}  



Consul-General Topakyan of Persia will give a Persian barbecue on Sunday at 2 P. M. at Persian Court, Morris Plains, N. J., in honor of ‘Abdu’l-Bahá of Persia.
